What is money, and why does it exist?

Before learning how to make money online, we need to start with the basics.

What is money?

Money is just a tool. It helps people exchange things.

That's it.

Before money existed, we traded things.

Meaning:

  • You give something
  • To get something else

Example:
I want a chicken — You want apples

So we agree: 1 chicken = 20 apples

This is called barter — the original way humans traded before currency existed.

Sounds okay... but here's the problem

What if:

  • You don't want apples?
  • You want a cow but I only have a knife?
  • I want bread but the baker wants a goat?

Everything becomes confusing and hard.

How do you trade:

  • A cow for shoes?
  • Half a cow for a phone?
  • A knife for a chicken?

Nobody knew what things were really worth. Trades were slow, unfair, and complicated.

So humans invented currency.

Money gives clear value to everything.

Instead of saying: "1 cow = 3 goats + 20 chickens"

We say:
👉 "1 cow = ,000"

Now it's simple:

  • Want a cow? Pay ,000
  • Want a chicken? Pay 0
  • Want a knife? Pay 5

Done ✅

Money is just a middle tool.
It avoids chaos.
It makes trading easy, fast, and fair.

But... What is a business?

Let's keep this very simple.

A business is when: you sell something to someone.

That's it. Nothing more.

You sell: a solution
To: someone with a problem

The more specific your niche, the easier it is to find and help the right people.

Here's how it works (very simple)

  • Someone has a problem
  • You know how to fix that problem
  • You create a product or a service to fix it
  • That person trusts you
  • They pay you

Why do people pay?

Why would someone give you money they worked hard for?

Simple answer:

People don't pay for products
They pay for results

People pay for change

People pay to go from:

  • Point A (where they are)
  • → Point B (where they want to be)

That's it.

But why don't they do it alone?

  • They don't know how
  • It's faster and easier to pay someone else

This is what makes conversion happen — when a curious person becomes a paying customer.

Very simple example

  • Point A: I'm hungry
  • Point B: I'm full and happy
  • Problem: I don't know how to cook
  • Solution: You cook for me

So I pay you.

You didn't sell food.
You sold the result.

This is the key idea

The harder it is to go from A to B,
the more people are willing to pay.

And: If your solution is faster, easier, and feels safe / guaranteed...

People will pay more, without hesitation.

How do you give them the solution?

In business there are 3 main families of ways to deliver a solution:

1. Product

You sell something. It can be:

  • Physical — like a T-shirt
  • Digital — like an eBook or a course

2. Service

You sell your time or your skills. Examples: Coaching, Freelancing, Helping someone do something.

3. Software

You create a tool. Examples: An app, A website. And people pay for that.

What's the best option for you?
